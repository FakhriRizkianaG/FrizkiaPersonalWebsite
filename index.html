About Me

Hi! My Name is Fakhri Rizkiana, i'm a writer and Illustrator based in Indonesia. I mainly draw anime style illustration using Clip Studio Paint. I mostly draw my own OC but i draw other stuff well.

I have about 3 years of experience in digital art. i started by using IbisPaint on my phone, then when i got my first pen tablet i switch over to Krita on my PC. Then after i got enough money, i switch over to Clip studio Paint and have been using it ever since.

In highschool i've become the coordinator of section 4 in the student council, an i also become the vice president of the Japanese Club.

I graduated from SMA Negeri 1 Cileunyi in 2024 and, i'm currently majoring in computer science in Universitas Pendidikan Indonesia.

The reason i pursued Computer Science is because i want to make a video game of my own. Since i love storytelling so much, and video game is one of the best storytelling media i've ever seen.

You can contact me on my email or you can go to my social media.
